About Me

My name is Max Jeon, and I am a pre-medical student turned pre-software engineer. I graduated from UCLA in 2018 with a Bachelor's Degree in Psychology with intention of pursuing a PhD, but decided to pivot into a career in medicine instead. COVID-related complications set my application process back two years, and when I was able to apply, I did not receive acceptance into the programs I was interested in. Throughout this process, I developed a growing interest in coding and because all of my closest friends are software engineers, I began to ask more and more questions about working in the field. I decided that a career as a software engineer was much better suited to my life goals, and I also found it to be much more interesting than I expected. I am now enrolled in a bootcamp called Rithm School and am excited to enter the software workforce.
